Why Ethereum Needs L2

Layer 1 Ethereum processes roughly 15 transactions per second. When demand spikes, bidding wars over gas fees break out, pushing transfer costs to tens of dollars. Layer 2 processes computations off-chain to bypass this bottleneck.

⚡

What is Arbitrum?

Arbitrum is an **Optimistic Rollup** scaling suite. It batches thousands of separate transactions together, executes them instantly off-chain, and rolls the highly compressed data back down onto Ethereum mainnet to secure it.

🌱

The Real-World Benefit

**Extreme Cost Efficiency.** Interacting with a DeFi market or minting an NFT directly on Ethereum can cost $20 in gas fees. On Arbitrum, that exact same action costs mere pennies ($0.01 – $0.05) with instantaneous confirmation.
